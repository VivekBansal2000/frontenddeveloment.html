What is Silver Stonks ?

Silver Stonks is a token built on top of Binance Smart Chain (BSC). We provide incentives to HODL (Hold On for Dear Life) unlike any other alt coin.

Our Mission

  • To educate people about the benefits of investing in silver.
  • To make investing in silver easy and fun.
  • To get each Silver Stonks token (SSTK) equal to the price of a gram of silver and maintain that price.

How Will We Get Each Silver Stonks Equal to A Gram of Silver?

RFI Static Rewards

Holders earn passive rewards through static reflection as they watch their balance of SafeMoon grow indefinitely.

Community Driven

Community driven & fair launch. The dev team burned all of their tokens and participated with everyone else.

Automatic LP

Every trade contributes towards automatically generating liquidity that goes into multiple pools used by exchanges

Tokken Narrative

Initial Supply: 1.74 Trillion

The initial supply of Silver Stonks tokens created represents the approximate amount of grams of silver that exist in the world. Since there is approximately 1.74 trillion grams of silver in the world, we created 1.74 trillion initial tokens.

Amount To Be Burned: 1.64 Trillion

Out of the 1.74 trillion grams of silver that exists, approximately 100 billion grams is .999 fine silver. The amount of tokens that remain after our manual burns that is representative of the amount of .999 fine silver that exists.
